Explore Tourist Places

Discover beautiful attractions and sightseeing spots in Rameswaram

Dhanushkodi Beach

Ghost town with pristine beaches and ruins. Where the Bay of Bengal meets the Indian Ocean. A must-visit for nature lovers.

18 km from Rameswaram 5:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Pamban Bridge

Iconic railway bridge connecting Rameswaram to mainland India. India's first sea bridge, offering spectacular views.

Connects Rameswaram to Mandapam Best viewed during sunrise/sunset

Kurusadai Island

Marine biological paradise with coral reefs and diverse marine life. Perfect for snorkeling and bird watching.

4 km from Mandapam Best time: Morning

Ariyaman Beach

Beautiful beach with clear blue waters, perfect for swimming and water sports. Well-maintained with facilities.

3 km from Rameswaram 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Gandamadana Parvatham

Highest point in Rameswaram offering panoramic views. Temple with footprints of Lord Rama, believed to be where he saw Lanka.

3 km from Rameswaram 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Adam's Bridge Viewpoint

Viewpoint to see the remains of Ram Setu (Adam's Bridge). Historical significance with beautiful natural scenery.

Dhanushkodi area Best time: Morning

Kalam Memorial House

Memorial museum dedicated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, former President of India. His childhood home converted into museum.

2 km from Rameswaram 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Five Faced Hanuman Temple Beach

Peaceful beach near Panchamukha Hanuman Temple. Perfect for meditation and witnessing beautiful sunrises.

Near Gandamadana Parvatham Open 24/7
