Temple Exploration Guide

Discover the sacred temples of Rameswaram with expert guidance

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlinga temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Famous for its long corridors and 22 holy wells (Theerthams).

5:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM Old Town, Rameswaram

Kothandaramaswamy Temple

Sacred temple located at Dhanushkodi, where Lord Rama performed coronation of Vibhishana. Offers stunning beach views.

6:00 AM - 7:00 PM Dhanushkodi, Rameswaram

Panchamukha Hanuman Temple

Unique temple featuring five-faced Hanuman statue. Located near the sea shore, known for floating stone beliefs.

6:00 AM - 8:00 PM Gandamadana Parvatham

Nambu Nayaki Amman Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Parvati, located near Ramanathaswamy Temple. Rich in history and architecture.

6:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM Near Ramanathaswamy Temple

Villondi Tirtham

Sacred water body where Lord Rama quenched Sita's thirst. Fresh water spring in the middle of sea, considered holy.

Open 24/7 7 km from Rameswaram

Agnitheertham

First of the 64 Theerthams, located 100 meters from Ramanathaswamy Temple. Popular for ritual bathing before temple visit.

Open 24/7 Near Ramanathaswamy Temple
